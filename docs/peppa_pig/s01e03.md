## Introduction

我叫做Peppa

而佢就係我細佬George

佢就係豬太太

佢係豬先生

我係Peppa

## Best Friend［最好的朋友］

Narrator: Peppa喺度等緊佢最好嘅朋友Suzy
<br><br><br>

[Door bell rings]

Peppa: Suzy，你好啊
<br><br><br>

Suzy: Peppa，你好
<br><br><br>

Narrator: Suzy係嚟揾Peppa一齊玩㗎.
<br><br><br>

Narrator: Peppa好鍾意Suzy. Suzy都好鍾意Peppa.
<br><br><br>

Narrator: 佢哋係最好嘅朋友
<br><br><br>

Mom: Peppa，不如你同Suzy上睡房玩吖？
<br><br><br>

Peppa: 好啊，媽咪
<br><br><br>

[Suzy and Peppa go upstairs to Peppa's bedroom. George looks sad.]

Narrator: George都想一齊玩喎
<br><br><br>

[Suzy and Peppa start playing with toys]

Narrator: Peppa同Suzy好鍾意喺Peppa間睡房度玩
<br><br><br>

[George joins in to play with some toys.]

Narrator: George都係啊
<br><br><br>

[Peppa walks up to George and takes the toy away from his hand.]

Peppa: 唔得啊，George. 呢個遊戲淨係摻女仔玩㗎咋
<br><br><br>

Peppa: 你去玩返你啲玩具啦
<br><br><br>

[Peppa walks away with the toy she just took from George]

Narrator: Peppa同Suzy淨係想自己玩咋
<br><br><br>

Peppa: 我就係小小嘅妖精公主
<br><br><br>

[Peppa, dressed as a fairy princess starts hopping around]

Peppa: 我要揮動我呢枝魔杖
<br><br><br>

Peppa: 將你變做隻青蛙！
<br><br><br>

[Girls proceed to play together and laugh together. George looks sad from far away from the girls.]

Narrator: George唔鍾意自己一個玩啊. George都想一齊玩啊
<br><br><br>

[George picks up another toy. Peppa walks up angrily to George]

Peppa: 唔得啊，George！我同Suzy玩緊㗎.
<br><br><br>

Peppa: 你要去第二度玩先得啊
<br><br><br>

[Peppa, again, takes the toy away from George]

Narrator: George想同Peppa一齊玩. 佢覺得有啲寂寞啊
<br><br><br>

[From downstairs in the kitchen, Mom calls out...]

Mom: George，我想你幫手. 我喺度整緊朱古力碎曲奇啊
<br><br><br>

[George happily goes down the stairs]

Mom: 我想你幫我𦧷乾淨個碗啊
<br><br><br>

[George proceeds to lick the chocolate off the bowl.]

Narrator: George好鍾意幫媽咪整曲奇㗎
<br><br><br>

Narrator: 但係佢更加鍾意同Peppa玩呢
<br><br><br>

[Girls continue to play upstairs]

Suzy: 我想做護士啊！
<br><br><br>

Peppa: 而我就想做醫生！
<br><br><br>

Suzy: 噉樣邊個做病人啊？
<br><br><br>

[Girls call out to George from upstairs]

Peppa: George！
<br><br><br>

Narrator: Peppa同Suzy好鍾意扮醫生同護士
<br><br><br>

Narrator: George都係啊
<br><br><br>

Narrator: Peppa聽緊George嘅心口
<br><br><br>

[George lays down in bed and Peppa walks up to him with a stethoscope]

Peppa: 嚟吖，George. 而家深呼吸，然後咳
<br><br><br>

[George coughs. Peppa thinks...]

Peppa: Hmm... 我覺得你個心有啲問題啊
<br><br><br>

Peppa: 我要幫你黏膠布
<br><br><br>

[Peppa puts a band-aid on George]

Suzy: 擘大個口吖，唔該
<br><br><br>

Narrator: 而Suzy幫George量體温
<br><br><br>

[Suzy puts the thermometer in George's mouth]

Suzy: 哎吔，弊喇，你燒得好緊要啊
<br><br><br>

Suzy: 我諗你需要入醫院，住三年啊
<br><br><br>

[George laughs. Dad walks in.]

Narrator: 豬先生入嚟揾George喎
<br><br><br>

Dad: 噢，弊喇，George佢咩事啊？
<br><br><br>

Peppa: 放心啦，我哋玩緊遊戲咋. George係我哋嘅病人
<br><br><br>

Dad: 哦，原來係噉. 噉我可唔可以探個病人啊？
<br><br><br>

Peppa: 你探一陣好喇. 病人要休息㗎
<br><br><br>

[Mom walks in with fresh cookies]

Suzy and Peppa: 曲奇啊！
<br><br><br>

Mom: 係啊，係畀George㗎. 呢啲係醫返好佢嘅藥嚟㗎
<br><br><br>

[Mom puts the cookies on George's lap in bed. George happily eats the cookies while the girls watch.]

Dad: 係喇，醫生，幫我檢查吖. 我個肚好痛啊
<br><br><br>

[Peppa puts the stethoscope on Dad's belly]

Dad: 好痕啊
<br><br><br>

Peppa: 我聽到你個肚打鼓啊
<br><br><br>

Peppa: 你一定係好肚餓喇，爹哋
<br><br><br>

Dad: 睇嚟我要食好多曲奇嚟醫返好佢啊
<br><br><br>

[Dad takes a cookie and all laugh.]

Mom: 我都係啊
<br><br><br>

Peppa: 我又係！
<br><br><br>

Suzy: 仲有我啊！
<br><br><br>

[Everyone eats cookies]
